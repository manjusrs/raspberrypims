1Five new commands

CommandWhat it doesExample
touchCreates a new empty filetouch day2.py
catPrints a file's contents to the screencat day2.py
cpCopies a filecp day2.py backup.py
mvMoves or renames a filemv backup.py old.py
rmDeletes a file, permanently, no trash canrm old.py
rm does not ask "are you sure." Once it's gone, it's gone. Always double check the filename before you press enter.

3Finishing conditionals

Quick recap: a conditional lets your code choose between different actions depending on whether something is true or false.

brightness = 0.8

if brightness > 0.5:
    print("bright")
elif brightness > 0:
    print("dim")
else:
    print("off")

elif means "else, if this other thing is true." You can chain as many as you need, and else catches everything left over.
